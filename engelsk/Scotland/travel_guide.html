Travel Guide

Throughout this course we have presented to you a small fraction of the cultural places, people, and events that make up Scotland and the Scottish people. There are so much more to Scotland, and it would be impossible to cover it all.

You now have the chance to show us what cultural artifacts and historical places you would visit if you were to travel around Scotland for 5 days.

Task

Make a Travel Guide:

Research 4 to 6 places or activities in Scotland that you may want to visit or try. Write down some keywords or phrases about each place or activity.

You should also note down why you want to visit that places, what’s special about them and why you find them a visit worthwhile.

You now must make a travel guide where you visit your researched places or activities.
