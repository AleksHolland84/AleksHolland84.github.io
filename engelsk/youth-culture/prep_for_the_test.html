Prep

Soon, you will have your final exams where your oral and written skills will be tested. Practise your skills and get ready by doing the task below

Final Task

Prep for the Test

Work in groups.

Each of you must make a presentation for the group and engage in a spontaneous conversation afterwards. Each presentation including the following conversation should take 5 minutes.

On your own:

  • Find at least two sources in English that talk about the youth culture and how youth have defined themselves within different generations.
  • Prepare keywords for your presentation.
  • Talk about what subculture is and try to define it. Discuss why you think people feel drawn to different subcultures. Consider; fashion and music style, activities, interests and values, the need to be part of a group and the need to rebel against e.g. parents. Do this for about 2 minute presentation.

In groups:

Give your 2 minute presentations in groups.

  • After 2 minutes, the group asks questions
  • Have a conversation about the differences between generation x, y and z for 3 minutes.
