The ESL Course - Teacher Guide

Forfatter: A. H. Johansen

Introduction to the Rick & Morty ESL Course

  • Start by introducing the show, its genre (science fiction/comedy), and the main characters (Rick and Morty).
  • Explain that students will watch selected episodes to improve their English language skills.

Episode Selection (15 minutes)

  • Choose one or two episodes with a balance of humor and sci-fi elements. Episodes like "Pickle Rick" and "Total Rickall" work well.
  • Provide a brief summary of the chosen episodes.

Pre-Watching Vocabulary (15 minutes)

  • Teach essential vocabulary related to the episodes, such as scientific terms, slang, and idiomatic expressions used in the show.
  • Create a vocabulary worksheet for students to complete while watching.

Watching the Episode(s) (24-60 minutes)

  • Show the selected episodes with English subtitles.
  • Encourage students to take notes on dialogue, new vocabulary, and interesting expressions.

Post-Watching Discussion (30 minutes)

  • Lead a discussion about the episodes, focusing on plot, character development, and moral dilemmas.
  • Encourage students to share their opinions and use new vocabulary in their responses.

ESL Tasks (30 - 45minutes)

Create ESL exercises related to the episodes. For example:

  • Comprehension questions.
  • Fill-in-the-blanks with missing words.
  • Role-playing exercises using dialogues from the episodes.
